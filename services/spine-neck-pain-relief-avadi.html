FAQs — Neck Pain & Tech-Neck

Do you treat tech-neck from phone/laptop use?

Yes. We run a focused tech-neck program with posture coaching, mobility work, and strengthening you can do at your desk.

Do I need an X-ray or MRI first?

Not always. Most neck pain improves without scans. We screen for red flags, then order imaging only if it changes management.

Will I get home exercises?

Yes. You’ll leave with a simple PDF and form cues; we progress it at follow-up.
