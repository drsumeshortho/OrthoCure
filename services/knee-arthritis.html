Knee Arthritis Care

At OrthoCure, knee arthritis is managed with a stepwise plan focused on exercise and lifestyle change first, medicines and physiotherapy next, and selected injections only when useful. Knee replacement is discussed calmly when all other options are exhausted.

What we assess

  • Pain on walking, stairs, or prolonged standing
  • Stiffness after rest
  • Swelling or grinding sensation
  • Impact on daily life and work
  • Weight, footwear, and lifestyle load

Early arthritis changes on X ray

When injections are useful

  • Persistent pain despite proper exercise
  • Early to moderate arthritis
  • Clear X ray correlation
  • Realistic expectations
