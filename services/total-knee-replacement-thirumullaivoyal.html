Frequently asked questions

When do you need total knee replacement
When pain stiffness and limits in daily life remain despite medication physiotherapy and injections and images show advanced wear or deformity.
What is recovery like
Most patients stand with support within one to two days and walk independently over four to eight weeks with guided physiotherapy and home work.
Is it safe if I have diabetes or a heart condition
Yes with proper workup hospital admission and routine monitoring. Plans are personalised after your fitness review.
How long will the implant last
Many implants work well for fifteen to twenty five years. Good weight control and regular follow up help long term results.
What about cost and insurance
Costs depend on implant type hospital and coverage. We provide clear written estimates during planning.
