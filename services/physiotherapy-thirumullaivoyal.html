Physiotherapy at OrthoCure Thirumullaivoyal

Physiotherapy at OrthoCure is built on one idea. The right exercise at the right time can do more for joints than any tablet. Plans are supervised, individual and linked to your orthopaedic diagnosis.

Call for physiotherapy appointment Book doctor and physio visit

Why physiotherapy matters

  • Reduces joint load and improves movement
  • Supports conservative care for arthritis and spine pain
  • Improves balance and confidence
  • Prevents long term stiffness after injury

Conditions we support

  • Knee arthritis and chronic knee pain
  • Shoulder pain and frozen shoulder
  • Spine and neck pain
  • Post fracture and post surgery stiffness

How your plan is created

  • Joint and movement assessment
  • Review of X rays and reports
  • Goal based exercise planning
  • Doctor and physiotherapist coordination

What a session looks like

  • Warm up and mobility
  • Strengthening exercises
  • Balance training
  • Stretching
  • Home exercise advice
  • Pain control guidance

Physiotherapy questions

Do I need to see a doctor first

Yes. A doctor review ensures fractures and serious causes are not missed.

How many sessions are needed

This depends on the condition and your goals. You will be given a realistic plan.

Can physiotherapy delay surgery

In many early and moderate cases it can delay or avoid surgery.
