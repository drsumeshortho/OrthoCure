What makes our physiotherapy different

  • One to one assessment with an orthopaedic surgeon and a physiotherapist
  • Strength, mobility and balance programs tailored to your goals
  • Support for joint pain, post fracture care, post surgery care, spine care and sports injuries
  • Clear home exercises with progress tracking and simple milestones
  • Evening and Sunday slots for working families

How your plan is built

  1. Visit us or book a consult for evaluation
  2. We set a plan that covers exercise, pain control and daily activity advice
  3. We review at regular intervals and progress step by step
  4. We guide your return to work, school and sport with safety checks

Frequently asked questions

Who needs physiotherapy
People who are recovering from a fracture or surgery, people with knee pain, back pain, neck pain, arthritis, sports injuries, or mobility and balance issues.
Is a doctor referral needed
No. You can book directly or walk in. The clinic is led by an orthopaedic surgeon who can assess you on the same visit.
Are sessions customised
Yes. Plans are personalised after evaluation. We explain every step in simple language and give home guidance.
What problems do you treat
Knee pain, back pain, shoulder pain, sports injuries, arthritis, post operative recovery, posture and balance problems.
