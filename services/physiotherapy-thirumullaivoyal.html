When should you seek urgent care?

If there is sudden weakness, loss of bladder control, severe chest pain, breathlessness, or severe injury with deformity, please visit a major hospital Emergency Room immediately.

Not sure if physiotherapy will help?

Many bone and joint problems improve with a structured rehabilitation plan, especially when the diagnosis is clear and the exercises are progressed safely.

  • Knee pain with stairs, sitting, or walking.
  • Back pain, sciatica type pain, or neck stiffness.
  • Sports injuries such as ankle sprains or ligament injuries.
  • Shoulder pain, frozen shoulder, or post injury stiffness.
  • Recovery after fractures once stability is confirmed.

We coordinate rehabilitation based on your examination findings. Progress is based on function, not only on pain.

How physiotherapy works here

Physiotherapy is not only heat packs and rest. It is a stepwise plan that reduces sensitivity, restores range of motion, and improves strength and control. We keep the plan practical and easy to follow for home and work routines in Thirumullaivoyal and nearby areas.

Step 1. Calm pain and swelling

Short term measures to settle flare ups and reduce protective muscle spasm.

Step 2. Restore range of motion

Gentle mobility work to reduce stiffness and improve joint movement safely.

Step 3. Build strength and control

Strength training and stability work to protect joints during walking, stairs, and work tasks.

Step 4. Return to activity

Gradual progression back to sport or daily routines, with clear do and do not guidance.

If Digital X ray is required for safety or diagnosis, we advise it during the visit.

When you should see the doctor before continuing exercises

Most people benefit from gentle movement. But if any of these are present, please get reviewed first:

  • Severe night pain that does not settle.
  • Progressive numbness, tingling, or weakness.
  • Major swelling after injury, or joint giving way.
  • Fever, recent major trauma, or unexplained weight loss.

Safety first. We keep rehab conservative and sensible.

Common questions?

Do I need a scan before physiotherapy?

Not always. Many conditions are diagnosed clinically. If imaging is needed for safety or clarity, Dr Sumesh will advise it after examination.

Will exercises increase my pain?

Mild discomfort can happen early, but exercises should not cause sharp worsening pain. We adjust the plan and progress stepwise.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on the condition and your routine. Some people improve in a few visits with home exercises. Chronic issues may need a longer plan. We guide you based on response.

Do you also guide sports injury rehab?

Yes. We coordinate rehabilitation for ankle sprains, knee ligament injuries, and shoulder instability patterns. For selected injuries needing surgery, we guide you clearly after examination.
