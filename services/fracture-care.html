Fracture Care in Thirumullaivoyal

Suspected fracture? Get a clear assessment, X-ray when required, proper immobilisation, and clear next steps — all in one visit.

Is it a fracture or just a sprain?

  • Did you hear a snap or crack at the time of injury?
  • Is there rapid swelling or bruising?
  • Does the limb look bent or out of shape?
  • Are you unable to put weight on it?
Clinic-based fracture assessment with plaster or splint support.

Common questions

Do you have X-ray facilities at the clinic?

Yes. Digital X-ray is available for patients being evaluated, only when it helps decision-making.

Do all fractures need surgery?

No. Many fractures heal well with plaster or splint support. Surgery is discussed only when clearly required.

How long does a cast usually stay on?

Most upper-limb fractures need around 4–6 weeks. Healing time depends on the bone and fracture type.
