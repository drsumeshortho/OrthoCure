Fracture care in Thirumullaivoyal Ambattur Avadi

Fractures need timely diagnosis and stable protection so bones heal in the correct position. At OrthoCure, fracture care focuses on accurate assessment, digital X ray only when required, proper immobilisation, and clear follow up guidance.

Go directly to a major hospital emergency room if the bone is visible through the skin, there is head injury, loss of consciousness, severe bleeding, or breathing difficulty.

Sprain or fracture

  • Severe pain after injury
  • Immediate swelling or bruising
  • Deformity or abnormal bending
  • Unable to bear weight or use the limb
We confirm the diagnosis with examination and digital X ray when it changes treatment.

How fractures are treated

  • Temporary splint to allow swelling to settle
  • Plaster of Paris cast for stable moulding
  • Fiberglass cast or brace when appropriate
  • Surgery discussed only when clearly needed

What to do before coming

  • Support and elevate the injured limb
  • Apply cold packs briefly
  • Do not attempt forceful correction
  • Carry any previous X rays if available

Common fracture care questions

Do all fractures need surgery

No. Many fractures heal well with proper plaster or splint support and regular follow up.

Do you have X ray at the clinic

Yes. Digital X ray is available when required to confirm diagnosis and guide treatment.

How long does a cast stay on

Most fractures require three to six weeks of immobilisation depending on the bone and injury.
