Fracture care for Ambattur, Avadi and Thirumullaivoyal

Fractures need timely diagnosis and firm protection so the bone heals in the correct position. At OrthoCure Bone and Joint Speciality Clinic, fracture care includes clinical assessment, digital X ray when required, and proper plaster, cast or splint support with structured follow up.

Why families choose OrthoCure

  • On site digital X ray when required
  • Precise plaster, cast or splint immobilisation
  • Clear recovery and follow up guidance
  • Convenient access for Ambattur and Avadi

Common fractures treated

  • Wrist and forearm fractures
  • Ankle and foot fractures
  • Clavicle fractures
  • Finger and hand fractures
  • Early stress fractures

What to do before coming

  • Elevate the injured limb
  • Use a cold pack briefly
  • Do not attempt self reduction
  • Carry any previous X rays

Fracture care questions

Do you have X ray at the clinic?

Yes. Digital X ray is available at the clinic when required for diagnosis.

Will every fracture need surgery?

No. Many fractures heal well with plaster or cast support without surgery.

How long does a cast stay on?

Most fractures require three to six weeks of immobilisation depending on the injury.
