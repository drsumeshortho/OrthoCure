Arthritis Treatment – Iyyappakkam

Personalised, conservative-first arthritis care by Dr. Sumesh Subramanian (MS Ortho). We diagnose accurately, reduce pain, protect joints, and build strength — so you can walk more, sleep better, and rely less on painkillers.

Why our approach works

  • Accurate staging: clinical exam + on-site digital X-ray when needed.
  • Pain control first: safe medication window; heat/ice; activity tweaks.
  • Joint protection: weight management, bracing if indicated, footwear advice.
  • Strength & mobility: targeted physiotherapy + home plan you can follow.
  • Injections when needed: hyaluronic acid / corticosteroid based on indication.

FAQs — Arthritis Treatment near Iyyappakkam

Do you treat without surgery?

Yes. Our approach is conservative-first — medication windows, lifestyle changes, physiotherapy, and injections when indicated.

Do I need an X-ray for arthritis?

Not always. We combine clinical exam with X-ray only when it helps decisions. Advanced scans are requested selectively.

Is Sunday open?

Yes, Sunday 11 AM–1 PM for reviews and follow-ups.

Will I get home exercises?

Yes. You’ll receive a progressive plan tailored to your stage and fitness, with check-ins to keep you on track.
