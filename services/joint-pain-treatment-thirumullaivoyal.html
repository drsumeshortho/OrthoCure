Joint Pain and Arthritis Treatment

Expert care for knee shoulder and other joint problems at OrthoCure Bone and Joint Clinic Thirumullaivoyal. We focus on modern non surgical therapy with clear guidance and measurable goals.

Knee Shoulder Hip Hand and wrist Foot and ankle

Why choose OrthoCure for joint pain

  • Detailed examination and precise diagnosis by a fellowship trained orthopaedic surgeon
  • Step wise plan that starts with advice exercise and pain control and adds injections only when useful
  • Platelet rich plasma and hyaluronic acid options when indicated with image guidance
  • Personal plans that aim for pain reduction better function and safe return to activity

How we treat joint pain and arthritis

  1. Consult and assessment of pain stiffness swelling and instability
  2. Diagnosis which may include X ray or selective blood tests
  3. Therapy which includes medication guided exercise physiotherapy and injections when indicated
  4. Follow up and progression based on your response

When joint replacement becomes the right next step

Most patients do well with non surgical care. When pain and disability remain high despite a full plan we discuss joint replacement in a clear and practical way.
