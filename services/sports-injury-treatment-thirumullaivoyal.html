  4. Return to sport graded steps with simple pass or hold checks.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need rest
Short rest can help, but early guided movement protects healing and speeds recovery. Full bed rest is rarely needed.
When do I need a scan
Scan is useful only if the result changes treatment. Many sprains recover well with a good plan and rehab.
How soon can I return to sport
Most people resume light activity in one to two weeks and sport in four to eight weeks based on strength and control tests.
