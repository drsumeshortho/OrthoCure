Service overview

Core orthopaedic services

These pages help patients understand what the clinic offers, what each service is meant for, and when in person examination matters.

Fracture care

Fracture Treatment

Immediate fracture assessment, plaster or splint care, follow up, and digital X ray support for quicker orthopaedic decision making.

 Sports injury

Sports Injury Treatment

Sprains, ligament injuries, meniscus problems, ankle instability, and shoulder instability with conservative first planning where reasonable.

 Diagnostics

Digital X Ray

On site digital imaging support so patients do not need to travel elsewhere before getting a clearer orthopaedic plan.

 Biologics

PRP Treatment

PRP may help selected early knee arthritis and selected tendon pain. Suitability is discussed after proper examination and realistic expectation setting.

 Shoulder

Frozen Shoulder Care

Stepwise care for stiffness and night pain with pain relief, movement recovery, and rehabilitation based planning.

 Rehabilitation

Physiotherapy

In house rehabilitation for pain relief, mobility, strength, fracture recovery, and safer return to daily activity.

 Joints

Knee Arthritis Care

Education, exercise, weight management, medicines, injections where appropriate, and conservative first knee care before discussing surgery.

 Surgery

Total Knee Replacement

For advanced arthritis where daily life is significantly blocked and non surgical care is no longer enough.

 Spine

Spine and Neck Care

Assessment of mechanical back pain, disc related symptoms, sciatica, tech neck, and conservative first spine care where appropriate.
