Who is knee replacement for?

Patients with advanced knee osteoarthritis who have persistent pain, stiffness, or deformity despite medication, injections, and physiotherapy.

Symptoms we look for

  • Pain with daily walking or climbing stairs
  • Night pain and morning stiffness
  • Severe bow-legs/knock-knees or frequent swelling

Our fast-track pathway

  1. Detailed consult & X-ray at clinic
  2. Personalised surgical planning
  3. Modern implants and balanced alignment
  4. Early mobilisation + targeted physiotherapy

Goal: safer surgery, less pain, faster return to walking.

FAQs — Knee Replacement in Ambattur

Do you provide X-rays on-site?

Yes. Our clinic has on-site digital X-ray so you get quick diagnosis without delays.

Is Sunday open for consultations?

Yes. We’re open on Sunday 11 AM–1 PM for pre-op and follow-up visits.

How long does recovery take?

Most patients walk the next day with support. Target: safe independent walking in 2–6 weeks, depending on fitness and physiotherapy.

What if I want to avoid surgery?

We always start with conservative options first — medication, braces, injections, and physiotherapy. Surgery is advised only when benefits clearly outweigh risks.
