Common shoulder problems we treat

  • Frozen shoulder (adhesive capsulitis) — stiff, painful, worse at night
  • Rotator cuff tendinopathy/partial tear — pain while lifting/reaching
  • Shoulder impingement — pain in arc, overhead activities
  • Calcific tendinitis — acute painful flare
  • Posture-related shoulder-neck strain

Our 3-phase protocol

  1. Phase 1: pain calm + protected mobility (capsular stretch, pendulums)
  2. Phase 2: rotator cuff + scapular strengthening with cues
  3. Phase 3: overhead control, load build, relapse prevention

Goal: lift, reach, and sleep without pain — safely and predictably.

FAQs — Shoulder Pain near Annanur

Do I need an MRI first?

Not usually. Most cases improve without MRI. We scan only if it changes treatment (e.g., suspected full tear or red flags).

Can you treat frozen shoulder without surgery?

Yes. We focus on pain control, capsular mobility, and progressive strengthening. Injections help selected patients.

How fast will I feel better?

Many feel relief within 1–2 weeks with the home plan. Full function depends on severity and adherence to rehab.

Is Sunday open?
