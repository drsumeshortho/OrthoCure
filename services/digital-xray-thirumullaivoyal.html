Digital X ray in Thirumullaivoyal Ambattur Avadi

Digital X ray at OrthoCure Bone and Joint Speciality Clinic supports quicker diagnosis for fractures and joint problems without sending you to another centre. Scans are advised only when they are truly needed and results are explained by Dr Sumesh Subramanian during the same visit whenever possible.

Why digital X ray at OrthoCure helps you

  • Same visit diagnosis for many fractures and joint problems.
  • No extra travel between clinic and X ray centre when you are already in pain.
  • Images reviewed directly with an orthopaedic surgeon, not in isolation.
  • Clear explanation of what the X ray shows and what it does not show.
  • Immediate start of plaster, splint or pain management when needed.

The X ray facility is for patients under care at OrthoCure. It is not run as a separate referral imaging centre.

When we usually advise an X ray

  • Falls or injuries where a fracture is suspected.
  • Persistent knee pain where arthritis needs confirmation or staging.
  • Joint swelling after injury that does not settle with rest.
  • Back or neck problems with red flag features where imaging will change the plan.
  • Follow up review of fractures already treated with plaster or surgery elsewhere.

Minor sprains and simple muscular pain are often managed without X ray after careful examination.

Inside our digital X ray room

Most studies take only a few minutes. Staff will help you move slowly and safely, especially after injury or surgery.

What to expect during your X ray visit

  • Clinical examination first to decide whether imaging is required.
  • Explanation of which views are planned and why.
  • Positioning with support to reduce pain as much as possible.
  • Digital images reviewed on screen with you in simple language.
  • Written plan for plaster, physiotherapy or further tests if needed.

Radiation safety and responsible use

  • Only required views are taken, guided by the clinical question.
  • Modern digital system allows lower exposure compared with older film units.
  • Extra care in children and women who may be pregnant, with shielding where appropriate.
  • No routine repeat films unless they change treatment decisions.
  • Images are stored safely for future comparison when you return for follow up.

Common questions about digital X ray

Do I always need an X ray for joint pain

No. Many soft tissue and muscular problems can be diagnosed with a good history and examination alone. X ray is advised when it is likely to change treatment, such as suspected fracture or arthritis.

Is digital X ray safe

Digital systems use controlled amounts of radiation. At OrthoCure only the required number of views are taken and extra care is used in children and in women who may be pregnant. For most adults the benefit of a needed X ray is far greater than the small risk.

Will you give me the X ray images to take elsewhere if needed

Yes. If you need review at another hospital for surgery or a second opinion, images can be shared or printed so that you do not have to repeat the test.

Can I walk in only for X ray without consultation

The facility is mainly for patients who consult at OrthoCure. If you are coming just for an X ray, a brief clinical review is still advised so that the right views are taken and the study is used meaningfully.
