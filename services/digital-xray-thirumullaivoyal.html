Frequently asked questions

Do I need a referral
No. Anyone can book or walk in for an X ray as part of an orthopaedic consult.
Is X ray safe for children and pregnancy
We use low dose settings and limit images in children to what is essential. For pregnancy we avoid X rays unless the benefit is clear after counseling.
How soon will I get the result
During the same visit the surgeon explains the image and gives a written or digital copy if you need one.
Which body parts do you image
Bone joint and spine for injury arthritis pre operative and post operative checks. For chest or abdomen we refer to a dedicated center.
