Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral?
No. You can call or WhatsApp directly. Walk-ins always welcome.
Are digital X-rays available on site?
Yes. Immediate imaging for faster, more accurate diagnosis.
Is surgery always required?
No. We use proven non-surgical options first; surgery only if it gives clear additional benefit.
Can fractures be treated at the clinic?
Yes. Injuries are stabilised with plaster or splint and surgery is arranged at trusted partner hospitals if needed.
