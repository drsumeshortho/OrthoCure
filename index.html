Frequently asked questions

Do I need a referral

No. You can call or use WhatsApp. Walk ins are welcome

Are digital X rays available on site

Yes. Quick imaging speeds up diagnosis and treatment

Is surgery always required

No. We begin with proven non surgical options and use surgery only when it improves outcome and function

Can fractures be treated at the clinic

Yes. We stabilise the injury with plaster or a splint and arrange surgery at partner hospitals when needed
