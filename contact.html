Meet Your Orthopaedic Expert

Dr. Sumesh Subramanian (MBBS, MS Ortho, Fellowship in Arthroplasty) is a trusted orthopaedic surgeon with a passion for evidence-based care, precision treatment, and patient education. With over a decade of clinical experience and academic involvement, he brings world-class orthopaedic solutions to his patients in Chennai and beyond.

He founded OrthoCure Bone & Joint Speciality Clinic with a vision to offer compassionate and accurate diagnosis, minimal intervention when possible, and a holistic approach to joint health and recovery.
