Clinical philosophy

Clear diagnosis. Conservative first. Stepwise escalation only when needed.

The treatment philosophy is straightforward: understand the symptoms, examine properly, use scans when they actually add value, and begin with the least invasive reasonable plan whenever appropriate. Surgery is a tool, not a default response.

Frequently asked

Common questions about consulting Dr Sumesh

What does an orthopaedic surgeon treat?
An orthopaedic surgeon treats bone, joint, ligament, tendon, spine, fracture, arthritis, and injury related problems.
Is surgery always the first option?
No. Conservative first orthopaedic care is preferred whenever reasonable. Surgery is discussed when it gives clear extra benefit.
Can I consult Dr Sumesh for a second opinion?
Yes. MRI, X ray, previous prescriptions, and prior surgery advice can all be reviewed during a second opinion consultation. See the Second Opinion page.
Are digital X rays available at the clinic?
Yes. Digital X ray support is available at OrthoCure Bone and Joint Speciality Clinic.
Do I need a referral?
No. You can call or WhatsApp directly. Walk ins are welcome during clinic hours, though calling ahead is helpful if you are carrying reports.
Where can I book an appointment?
You can visit the Consult page to request an appointment and share your concern.
