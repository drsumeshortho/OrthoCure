Meet Dr. Sumesh Subramanian

Dr. Sumesh Subramanian (MBBS, MS Ortho, Fellowship in Arthroplasty) is a trusted orthopaedic surgeon with deep clinical expertise and a grounded, empathetic approach to healing. Founder of OrthoCure Bone & Joint Speciality Clinic, he offers advanced bone and joint care rooted in evidence-based medicine and tailored to the needs of every patient.

Our Philosophy

At OrthoCure, we believe that healing begins with trust, movement, and precision. Whether it's arthritis, back pain, fractures, or rehab, our clinic is elder-friendly, family-focused, and backed by modern tools like physiotherapy and digital X-ray. We aim to be a true health ally for your bone and joint journey.

Dr. Sumesh is an active member of the Indian Orthopaedic Association (IOA) and Tamil Nadu Orthopaedic Association (TNOA), maintaining the highest standards in orthopaedic care through continuous learning, compassionate service, and ethical practice.
