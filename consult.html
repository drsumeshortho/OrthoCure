Acute Injury or Severe Pain Outside Clinic Hours?

If any of the following apply, please proceed directly to the nearest hospital casualty — do not wait for an online appointment:

  • Sudden severe pain following a fall or accident
  • Visible deformity of a limb or joint
  • Inability to bear weight or move a limb
  • Rapid swelling and bruising after trauma
  • Open wound near a joint or bone
🏥  Go to casualty for a basic assessment and X-rays first. Dr. Sumesh can review your reports and coordinate further orthopaedic management once you are stable.
